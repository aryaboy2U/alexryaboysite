Hello, and thank you for stopping by. My name is Alex, and I’m a composer, songwriter, producer and sound designer. I write music for theater, TV, and podcasts. My main vehicle for songwriting is my band Blue Circus. I also like collaborating with other artists to guide and capture their creative vision. In addition to music, I’m passionate about theater and I’m one of the creators and performers of a theatrical clown piece Le Grand Voyage. I’m always looking for fun and interesting projects, so feel free to drop me a line.
